Participate in the CDR Plus Initiative

CDR+ is an open source initiative that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We'd love you to join us! Here's a few ways to find out what's happening and get involved. {{% /blocks/lead %}}

Connect

Using or want to use CDR Plus? Find out more here:

  • Slack: Connect with Consumer Data Right implementers
  • hello@cdrplus.au Connect with the CDR+ coordinators
  • Consumer Data Standards: Access the underlying Data Standards
  • Data Standards: DSB Standards Maintenance Iterations

Contribute

If you want to get more involved by contributing to CDR Plus, join us here:

  • CDR+ on GitHub: Repositories forming the CDR Plus initative

You can find out how to contribute to these docs in our Contribution Guidelines

We hold a weekly meeting between 2-3pm every Thursday in #general on the CDR Plus Slack
